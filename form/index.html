Medical and health conditions

Massage treatments can be affected by certain health conditions and in some cases may:

  • Require clients to receive consent from a GP or health professional before receiving treatment.
  • Require adjustments to treatments.
  • Prevent clients from receiving treatment entirely.

Any changes to a treatment will be discussed with the therapist. It is recommended that clients receiving medical care should inform their GP or health professional of their massage treatments even if consent is not required.

Cancellations

To cancel or reschedule a treatment you've booked online, simply follow the link found in your confirmation email. Otherwise please contact us on 07752 463226.

  • Cancellations must be made at least 4 hours prior to a treatment.
  • 50% treatment fee will be charged for cancellations made with less than 4 hours notice.
  • 100% treatment fee will be charged if the client does not attend their treatment.
Terminations

The therapist will refuse or cancel a treatment if:

  • The treatment is not deemed safe or suitable for the client – no fee.
  • The room or environment is unsuitable for carrying out the treatment – 50% treatment fee.
  • The client is intoxicated or under the influence of drugs – 100% treatment fee.
  • The client demonstrates inappropriate behaviour or mistreatment towards the therapist – 100% treatment fee.
Payment
  • Each treatment fee is quoted and agreed upon during the booking process.
  • Payment can be made online or at the time of treatment with cash or card.
  • Payment must be provided in full by the end of the treatment (unless received beforehand).
  • In the event that a treatment is cut short by the client, the full treatment fee will still be charged.
