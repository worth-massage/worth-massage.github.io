Our Treatment Room

Surrounded by beautiful countryside, our treatment room in Rottingdean is the perfect space for your massage and is well equipped to ensure your treatment ticks all the boxes. Located just a pebble's throw away from Brighton, we have plenty of free parking available nearby and are easily accessible via the 2/2A bus so visiting us couldn't be easier.

Our Mobile Service

No need to leave the house; our mobile service saves you the time and hassle of travelling to and from your massage treatment. All the necessary equipment is provided by us allowing you to sit back, kick your feet up and enjoy a truly effortless experience. We operate within Brighton & Hove and the surrounding area as seen on our map, however for those of you that are located outside of our area, please feel free to get in touch regardless as exceptions can be made.
